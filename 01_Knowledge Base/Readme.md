## Knowledge Base

This folder contains the knowledge base used by the CAPI. The dataset consists of two columns:

* **Question**: A user query or information request.
* **Answer**: The corresponding reference answer used as the chatbot's knowledge source.

The complete knowledge base comprises **291 question–answer (QA) pairs**, curated from multiple authoritative sources, including:

* The Informatics Study Program curriculum and academic documents.
* University regulations and academic guidelines.
* Relevant regulations and policies issued by the Government of Indonesia.

To protect institutional documents and comply with copyright and data-sharing considerations, **only a subset of the knowledge base is included in this repository**. The released samples are intended solely to illustrate the dataset structure and support the reproducibility of the proposed methodology, rather than to provide the complete knowledge base used in the experiments.
